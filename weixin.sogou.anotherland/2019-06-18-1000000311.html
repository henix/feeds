家园计划AnotherLand”是一个涉及生态环保和人道主义的民间公益计划。该计划致力于对科技、经济、文化、教育领域进行反思,并为因次衍生的贫富差距等问题展开实际行动

 

唐冠华世界生活方式贡献奖（TLCA）

TangGuanhua Lifestyle Contribution Awards

http://Awards.TangGuanhua.com


TLCA颁发给对人类生活方式有持续性和代表性贡献的个人、团体和作品。

该奖项设立目的在于鼓励人类追求自由、平等与幸福，增进人与人，人对世界、对生活的理解。最终评审权和一切解释权属于唐冠华个人，由该奖项产生的争议、影响、资源属于社会。

TLCA is awarded to individuals, groups and works that have a sustained and representative contribution to the human lifestyle.


The purpose of the award is to encourage humanity to pursue freedom, equality and happiness,Promote people and people, people's understanding of the world and life.The final review right and all interpretation rights belong to Tang Guanhua personally. The controversy, influence and resources generated by the award belong to the society.



这个奖项注定是挂一漏万的，这证明人间无名的奉献从未停止。


我要谈的是通过一个奖项，让我们所知的：让社会得以多元化、为人类彼此尊重作出行动或毕生努力、敢于提出并持续践行独树一帜生活观念的同伴，得到更多、更深的重视和讨论。也让更多无名奉献的同伴被知道。因为正是这些同类在通过点滴的努力，坚守我们所自称的物种：人类。


我相信我们的灵魂因这神秘又必然的使命而不朽。


希望借此奖项提醒自己：来到这个世界的意义，是为了追求自由、平等与幸福。

This award is destined to be a hangover, which proves that the nameless dedication of the world has never stopped.


What I want to talk about is an award, let us know: to make society more diversified, to act for human respect or to work hard, to dare to propose and continue to practice the unique concept of life, to get more and deeper attention And discussion. Also let more unnamed companions be known. Because it is these same kind of efforts in the bit by bit, stick to what we call the species: human.


I believe that our soul is immortal because of this mysterious and inevitable mission.


I hope to remind myself that the significance of coming to this world is to pursue freedom, equality and happiness.


唐冠华TangGuanhua

2020年候选人已确定，现接受2022年提名。

欢迎任何人自荐或推荐（TLCA）候选人或提名作品，提名可参考（不限于）以下六项标准。可将个人资料、肖像照片、作品介绍等发往邮箱guanhua75@163.com


Candidates for 2020 have been confirmed and are now nominating for 2022.We welcome any person to recommend (TLCA) candidates or nominated works. Nominations can refer to (not limited to) the following six criteria.Send your personal information, portrait photos, and work descriptions to guanhua75@163.com


1.Advance 超 前 性

参选人所触及的问题对未来和下一代的意义相对而言比对当下和个人的意义和效果更大，通过率先行动促进制度和律法进一步完善。

The issues touched by the candidates are relatively more meaningful to the future and the next generation than to the present and the individual. Promote the further improvement of the system and the law through its own initiative.


2.Educational experience 教 育 经 历

未经历普遍学院教育体系，却具有独到的知识见解和解决社会问题的思路。

Candidates do not experience the general college education system, but have unique knowledge insights and ideas for solving social problems.


3.Persistence 持 续 性

参选人曾连续十年以上践行某种与大众不同的价值观、审美观或世界观。

The candidate has practiced a different value, aesthetic or worldview that is different from the public for more than ten years.Representation: There is an irreplaceable personality or creative difference compared to other similarities in the era or space.


4.Living environment 生 存 环 境

不具备优渥的经济条件和优越的生存环境，却创造出难以估量的精神、文化价值。

The candidate does not have excellent economic conditions and superior living environment, but creates an incalculable spiritual and cultural value.


5.Influence 影 响 力

行动与思想在全球传播，并引起广泛议论，相关信息的传递触及多种领域和媒介。

Actions and ideas are spread around the world and cause widespread discussion. The transmission of relevant information touches various fields and media.


6.Representation 代 表 性

在所处时代或空间中与其他同类相比，存在难以替代的人格或创作差异。

There is an irreplaceable personality or creative difference compared to other similarities in the era or space.
