The power of Narrative

Narrative为什么重要？

往小了讲Narrative会最终影响自己阅读市场、交易市场的方式。

往大了讲，背后可以产生一整套思维逻辑框架，最终影响到市场的微观结构。比如同质化严重的被动型ETF都已经开始威胁到整个金融体系了.

个人很喜欢上善若水的一个演讲《不确定市场中的确定性收益》表述：

对市场本质认识的深刻程度，很大程度能决定投资业绩的前景和可持续性。对市场的认识有很多种，每一种都发展出了不同的投资哲学，甚至影响了产业链。比如，很多人都知道“有效市场假说”，ETF的设计其实就是基于这个假说出发的，确实大部分主动型管理基金就是跑不过市场，它的出现能让你跟得上市场，这足以打败70%以上的基金，慢慢的它就演变成一个庞大的产品类别。
我这里指的是总体市场，一个宏观的、全局的市场，但并不是说不存在“确定的市场”，局部的、片刻的市场确实存在着确定性。
不可能仅用一种方法能解释全局的市场，也不可能用一成不变的模式来解释发展变化的全局市场。进一步的，我们可以认为，任何一种认识只能解释部分市场特性，任何一种方法也只能抓住部分机会。

我自己平时做交易也是努力顺着这个思路来的，时刻去思考市场当中哪些是不确定的，是自己不能把握的，也就是不在自己能力范围内的。这样的好处是错过了没有丝毫心理压力，可以以崭新的精神面貌，面对未来新的行情。有朋友问我，没吃到那波突然的行情，后不后悔？我说不后悔，首先不在我的能力范围内，再者，这个市场不确定性的呈现，给了我布局接下来要进行的行情更加多的确定性。比如11月24日的行情直接贡献了12月21日的利润。

又比如我坚信日内均值回归的力量：

I don't know when,

when will a trend start and when will it end;

But I know for sure,

that it can't last forever will revert in the end with good odds.

Go for the jugular.

Adaptive Markets Hypothesis,

It takes a theory to beat another theory (EMH).

Paraphrase from 《Efficiently Inefficient》

Financial evolution proceeds at the speed of thought, where several generations of ideas can come and go within the time span of a productive working lunch.
The price discovery process isn't free, and in the absence of economic incentives— in other words, profit opportunities, also known as market inefficiencies— liquid financial markets will simply cease to exist.

Quant is just another form of heuristics

The Adaptive Markets Hypothesis elegantly resolves these difficulties by first observing that prices don't automatically reflect all available information— how could they? We've noted that buyers and sellers don't use all available information to make their decisions— they use some information and heuristics instead. These heuristics can be extraordinarily sophisticated, like the quantitative strategies of Shaw and Simons, but they're still heuristics. As markets become more competitive, however, investors have to adapt their heuristics to maintain a profit.

On AI+ML & market efficiency

Adaptive Markets Hypothesis includes the Efficient Markets Hypothesis as a special case. Unlike the Efficient Markets Hypothesis, the Adaptive Markets Hypothesis makes no claim that markets will always become more efficient over time. Instead, it predicts more complicated market dynamics. Just as some species on Earth have become extinct while new ones emerge, the history of financial markets has been punctuated by crashes, panics, manias, bubbles, and other natural market phenomena. Any trend toward greater efficiency in financial markets is a product of human intellect applied to the marketplace— evolution at the speed of thought— not necessarily a consequence of the marketplace itself.

Nice summary on HF：

A hedge fund is a private partnership that has a start and an end, and involves a general partner and several limited partners, each of whom brings something to the partnership. At the start, the general partner brings all the experience and limited partners bring all the money. At the end, the general partner leaves with all the money and the limited partners leave with all the experience. At its core, it's simply a private partnership.
