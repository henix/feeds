摘要

本文首次提供了受大数据驱动的金融科技影响企业动态的证据。本文用阿里巴巴（中国最大的电子商务平台和金融科技贷款机构）的综合数据，检验了金融科技信贷对小企业规模分布的因果性影响。研究发现，平台信贷促进了企业选择——信贷促使规模更大、声誉更好的电商的市场份额更快增长。为揭示信贷传导机制，研究了信贷对不同行业产品定价的影响。本文还发现了一个动态效应：用平台专有算法计算的公司信用评分与其市场份额和声誉高度相关，由此说明，公司市场地位和金融科技信贷之间的反馈循环加速了公司选择过程，促进了企业成长。


Fintech and Firm Selection: Evidence from E-commerce Platform Lending


Abstract

This paper provides the first evidence on how fintech powered by big data impacts firm dynamics. With comprehensive data from Alibaba, the largest e-commerce platform and the largest fintech lender in China, we estimate the causal impact of fintech credit on the size distribution of small businesses. We find that platform credit promotes firm selection – credit leads to stronger growth of market share for e-commerce merchants that are larger and more reputable. To unveil the credit transmission mechanism, we examine the impact of credit under different industry conditions and the impact on product pricing. We also document a dynamic effect: firms’ credit score, assigned by platform's proprietary algorithm, is highly correlated with their market share and reputation, so a feedback loop between firms’ market status and fintech credit that speeds up the process of firm selection in this fast growing entrepreneurial space.  


原文：

Huang, Yi, Ye Li and Hongzhe Shan, Fintech and Firm Selection: Evidence from E-commerce Platform Lending, 2019 AFA Annual Meeting Working Paper, May 1, 2018.
